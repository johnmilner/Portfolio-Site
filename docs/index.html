About

John Milner is a web developer and digital creative based in San Diego, California. He has award-winning experience working across global and regional campaigns for clients such as Nike, Nokia, Axe (Lynx), Bud Light, Quaker, and Ad Council.

He is well-versed across all platforms and believes emotion and story are fundamental to creating fresh brand-driven content and interactions. His work bridges culture, design and technology from interactive, mobile and social to gaming, film and television -- with a focus on creating holistic, engaging and entertaining brand experiences.
